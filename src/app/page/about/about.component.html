Interesting Attractions

China (ประเทศจีน)

Great Wall Of China (กำแพงเมืองจีน)

     The Great Wall of China was built more than 2,500 years ago with the goal of protecting the North from Mongolian and Turkic invasions. The Great Wall of China is 21,196 kilometers long and covers more than 15 provinces across China. It looks like a long wall with a fort separating it.

Peru (ประเทศเปรู) Machu Picchu (มาชูปิกชู)

     Machu Picchu is located about 70 kilometers northwest of Cusco. Inside are the ruins of temples, palaces, fountains and altars, and other intricate pieces of architecture. The explorers have invented many theories for the construction of Machu Picchu, for example, to worship God. as a vacation home for the Inca emperor It is an agricultural experimental station. It is a place for rituals related to astronomy, etc. The highlight of Machu Picchu is at the Guardhouse, where friends can see a beautiful panoramic view of Machu Picchu. However, visiting Machu Picchu to get more enjoyment should go during the winter months from May to October. Because it's a rain-free period, make travel comfortable.

India (ประเทศอินเดีย) Taj Mahal (ทัชมาฮาล)

     The Taj Mahal is the only modern wonder of the world born of the power of love. It is both a monument to love and timeless architecture. Located in Agra India Made from pure white marble and laterite. adorned with many jewels To be used as a burial monument of the emperor's wife, Shah Jahan, who loved each other for 18 years.
The Taj Mahal is 42 acres in size and took 22 years to build. The best time to visit is during sunrise. Because the sky will be a beautiful pink cool weather and people are not crowded
